About Me

I'm SummerLemon, a contemporary drawing artist based in Philippines, Quezon City. For the past years, I've been exploring the boundaries between traditional drawing techniques and modern artistic expression.

My work often focuses on the interplay between light and shadow, finding beauty in everyday moments and translating emotions into visual stories. I work primarily with charcoal, graphite, and colored pencils, occasionally incorporating watercolor and ink.

After graduating from the Portland Institute of Fine Arts in 2015, I've exhibited my work in galleries across the United States and Europe. My drawings have been featured in various art publications and have received recognition for their distinctive style and emotional depth.

When I'm not in my studio, I teach drawing workshops and mentor emerging artists, sharing my passion for the medium and its endless possibilities.
